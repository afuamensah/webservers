Zara announces launch of new makeup collection for Christmas

Reposted from the Independent

Zara has announced the release of a brand new cosmetics line just in time for your Christmas shopping.

The makeup range has been inspired by the dramatic looks devised by Vogue beauty editor-at-large Pat McGrath for the Zara autumn/winter 2018 campaign, which was shot by Steven Meisel.

Zara has followed in the footsteps of other high street retailers such as Topshop and & Other Stories in launching a makeup line that will likely prove popular among consumers.
